Key Takeaways
    • There are three clusters:
    1. Apps with low rating low installs.
    2. Apps with high rating low installs.

  • Apps with low rating shall focus on increasing their app's user experience.
Installs vs. Price and Rating
{% autoescape off %} {{plot_3d}} {% endautoescape %}
Key Takeaways
    • There are four clusters (Analysis only for paid apps):
    1. Highly installed apps
    2. Highly priced apps
    3. Lowly priced apps with lower rating.
    4. Lowly priced apps with higher rating (which also happens to get more installs).
  • Highly install apps could hike their prices to gain more profits.
  • Highly priced apps shall lower their prices to increase their user base.
  • Lowly priced apps with lower rating must either make their app free of focus on increasing the user experience and meet the expectations of the user.
  • Lowly priced apps with higher rating shall focus more on marketing and increasing their user base.
